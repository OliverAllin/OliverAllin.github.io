Project Outline

Group Business Design Project

Summary

As part of my final year at the University, we were placed in groups and paired with an industrial supervisor, who tasked us with designing a refugee shelter for the United Nation. The shelter would be made up of majority PLASTECO’s Smartawood materials and should aim to compete with the UNs existing solutions. During this project I was the design lead, walking the group through different ideation and design methods to produce a product that was feasible to the design requirements and unique.

My report for the project explores the designing of the Base. I aimed to create a universal, modular, and structurally sound base for the shelter to be built upon. The base is made of PLASTECO’s Smartawood (Plastecowood, n.d.) and allows for the variation in shelter shape and size. The Bases’ weight, size and cost were a few of the deciding variables used in designing the base. The Base is assumed to be built on flat ground in a hot climate.
