About Me

A diligent, dedicated, and conscientious graduate from the University of Bath, graduating with a 2.1 in Integrated Design Engineering. I have a personal goal to pursue a career in the automotive industry. Attracted to problem solving, seeking to produce the best possible solution to the specific challenge, and working under pressure have been decision drivers for me to be involved in a highly demanding environment.I am passionate about high performance cars and an avid follower of Formula 1 and Motor Grand Prix.
