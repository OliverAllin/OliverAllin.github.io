Design and Make

In my second year of university, we were put into groups and tasked with designing a mechanism to launch a ping pong ball across a ping pong table. The mechanism had to be able to launch the ball and have it land in each third of the oppositions side of the table. It also had to be able to be reloaded and fired again whilst changing its angle of launch. For this design we used a morphological chart to decide on the best design for each part of the mechanism. I was in charge the ideation of the loading mechanism. Deciding on the using a gravity loaded mechanism to drop the ball into the firing chamber as it was the most efficient and simple design. The final design also included a rack and pinion system to change the angle of launch and a spring/elastic band firing mechanism.

Business Case Study

As part of my final year of university, I was tasked with writing a business case study on a company of my choice. I chose to write about McMurtry Automotive, a company that designs and manufactures the worlds fastest single seater Electric car. The case study included research into what the company does, why it does that, how it does it, what strategy it uses and how we could improve the company. The case study was a great way to learn about how a company works. From this project I achieved a grade of 80.
